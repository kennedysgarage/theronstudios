Katalyst Live

A known name in NYC tech scene is Haithem Elembaby. So when Haithem reached out to us to design a logo for his new venture, Katalyst Live we were excited to work together. Haithem provided us with some ideas and guidelines for what his company represents, this gave us some direction on the design.

Services

  • Identity

Logo

Haithem is known for his amazing events that he puts on here in NYC. We figured that Katalyst would be an incredible company that would put on larger than life events with amazing experiences. The final version of their logo was inspired by two stage lights crossing paths, this representing Katalyst collaborating with the company they are working with.
