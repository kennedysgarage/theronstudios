About us

Theron Industries is a NYC based development studio. We offer design, development and branding services. Our team is adept at creating high end user experiences resulting in frequent use with long user engagement. We create consistent branding and user experiences across web, native mobile and wearable technologies, allowing your users to transition from one medium to another seamlessly.
